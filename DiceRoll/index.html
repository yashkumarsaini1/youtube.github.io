Rules for game.
  • This is a two player game.
  • Who score 100 first will win the game.
  • Roll Buttons Active with the current players chance.
  • Start by clicking respective roll buttons.
  • Player 1 will start first.
  • If you got " 1 " on dice then the score reduced to the saved score.
  • Any player can save score anytime and pass chance to the other player.
  • Initial Saved Score is 0.
  • The numbers on the dice will added to the score.
