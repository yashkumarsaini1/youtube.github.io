FRP

Fibure FRP, is a composite material made of a polymer matrix reinforced with fiberglass, used as a central strength member in fiber optic cables.

IGFR

IGFR has a special smooth, flexible coating which improves handling and eliminates dust and loose fiber generation during cable production even at high speeds, leading to high productivity and ultimate cable strength.

ARAMID RIPCORD

Fibure Aramid Ripcord is an aramid based, continuous filament yarn designed specifically for fiber optic cable ripcord application. Fibure Aramid Ripcord for ripping through steel armour or PE Jacket.

POLYESTER RIPCORD

Fibure Polyester Ripcord is polyester based, continuous filament yarn designed specifically for fiber optic cable ripcord application.They are for the easy removal of PE Jackets.

Binder yarn

Fibure Binder Yarn is made of excellent polyester which ensures the constant elongation properties, the optimum winding tension ensures high rotational speed.

Water Swellable Yarn

Fibure Water Swellable Yarn is ideal for use inside the cable as it swells instantly when it comes in contact with water and prevents the cable from damage.
