FRP

Fibure FRP, is a composite material made of a polymer matrix reinforced with fiberglass, used as a central strength member in fiber optic cables.

IGFR

IGFR has a special smooth, flexible coating which improves handling and eliminates dust and loose fiber generation during cable production even at high speeds, leading to high productivity and ultimate cable strength.

ARAMID RIPCORD

Fibure Aramid Ripcord is an aramid based, continuous filament yarn designed specifically for fiber optic cable ripcord application. Fibure Aramid Ripcord for ripping through steel armour or PE Jacket.
