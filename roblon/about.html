Indore is a world leader in cable protection solutions. We at Indore make products that provide protection and support to fiber optic and power cables in terms of load bearing capabilities ,water ingress and insulation. Our vast experience, our people, our manufacturing backbone, our culture of innovation and service has earned us the most valuable and treasured asset the customers trust. In many ways we play a silent role in keeping our world connected We keep cables protected from every stress imaginable, So let’s begin our collaboration to protect our cables today.
